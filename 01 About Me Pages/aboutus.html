About Us

Meet the Brearley School's computer science club! We attend competitions and do fun coding projects with our community of inclusivity and involvement. We also help host the Brearley School's annual coding competition, BevHacks. But along with that we hope to do more interschool activities in the future and later in the school year, in the spring. We encourage all high school students to pop in sometime and discover the wonders of the Computer Science club!
